## Model Prediction Behaviour

The multiple regression model achieved an R-squared value of **0.811396**, indicating that it explains approximately **81.14%** of the variation in monthly sales.

The presence of both large positive and large negative residuals shows that while the model performs well overall, some individual stores behave differently from model expectations. This indicates that additional business factors, beyond marketing spend, footfall, inventory availability, and region, may also influence sales performance.

Overall, the model provides strong predictive performance and is suitable for business decision-making, although it should not be treated as a perfect predictor for every individual store.
